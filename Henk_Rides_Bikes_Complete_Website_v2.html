About

Henk Rides Bikes is about adventure, curiosity, craftsmanship, honesty, resourcefulness, connection and humor.

What it is

Using motorcycles to explore places, build things, meet people and collect stories — while figuring it out as I go.

What it is not

No showroom builds. No fake influencer perfection. No cheap reclame for the sake of reclame.

What works

Sometimes the solution is custom-built. Sometimes it is AliExpress. The point is not price. The point is honesty.

Core values

  • Adventure
  • Curiosity
  • Craftsmanship
  • Authenticity
  • Resourcefulness
  • Connection
  • Humor
